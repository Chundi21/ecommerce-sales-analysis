 E-Commerce Sales Analysis

Overview
Analysis of retail e-commerce transaction data covering $105M in total 
sales across 9,191 customers and 11 countries using Microsoft Excel 
pivot tables and data analysis techniques.

 Key Findings
- Total revenue: $105.4M across 10 months of data
- 16.1% year-over-year growth in both sales and quantity — 
  entirely volume-driven, average price remained flat at $17.56
- Card payments dominate at 90% of transactions ($94.6M) vs 
  mobile at 8% and cash at 3%
- Dhaka is the top-performing district at $40.7M (39% of total revenue)
- 4th week sales spike to $33M vs ~$24M average for other weeks — 
  flagged as a pattern for further investigation
- Bangladesh and India are the top countries by revenue
- Top supplier DENIMACH LTD contributing $13.3M in sales
- Canned products lead in quantity at 1.3M units sold

 Business Insights
- Volume-driven growth with flat pricing suggests market expansion 
  rather than price inflation 
- 4th week spike likely driven by end-of-month bulk purchasing behavior
- Card payment dominance at 90% indicates a strong digital payment 
  adoption in the customer base
- Geographic concentration in Dhaka (39%) presents both opportunity 
  and risk — overdependence on one market

Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Data Analysis)
